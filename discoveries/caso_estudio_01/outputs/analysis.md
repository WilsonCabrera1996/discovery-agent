# Discovery Analysis: Caso de Estudio 01 - Clínica Médica

## Pain Points

### For Patients
- **Accessibility:** Limited to calling during office hours, which conflicts with their own work schedules.
- **Reliability:** Risk of "lost" appointments due to manual recording errors, leading to long wait times.
- **Retention:** Patients often forget appointments or lose physical reminder cards.

### For the Clinic (Receptionist)
- **Inefficiency:** High volume of repetitive "FAQ" calls distracts from in-person patient care.
- **Human Error:** Maintaining a dual system (Notebook + Excel) leads to synchronization failures and double-bookings.
- **Manual Labor:** 1.5 hours per day is wasted on manual reminder calls.

## Current Workarounds
- Patients call repeatedly during lunch breaks or take time off work to manage appointments.
- Receptionists manually call 30+ people every afternoon.
- Reliance on "unstable" manual record-keeping (Notebook/Excel).

## Opportunities
- **24/7 Self-Service Booking:** Allow patients to view availability and book at their convenience.
- **Automated WhatsApp Integration:** Send instant confirmations and automated reminders to reduce no-shows.
- **Unified Digital Agenda:** A single source of truth for the clinic to prevent scheduling conflicts.

## Hypotheses

### Hypothesis 1 (Communication)
We believe that **implementing automated WhatsApp reminders** for **patients and the receptionist** will result in **a 50%+ reduction in manual phone work and fewer missed appointments**.

### Hypothesis 2 (Scheduling)
We believe that **providing a web-based self-service booking portal** for **patients** will result in **fewer repetitive phone inquiries and a higher rate of successful bookings made outside office hours**.
